What Is This

Numerology as Source Code

The universe runs on mathematics. Your name and birth date are not coincidences — they are encoded data, parameters set at the moment your simulation began. Numerology is the practice of reading that code.

Simulation Source Code goes deeper than traditional numerology, revealing seven distinct frequencies that together form the complete architecture of your experience — your drives, your gifts, your lessons, your destiny.

About

Simulation Source Code

A framework for understanding the numerical architecture encoded in your name, birth, and existence.

· The Philosophy ·

Simulation Source Code began with a simple question: what if numerology isn't mysticism — what if it's mathematics? What if the numbers encoded in your birth and name are not symbolic but literal? Not metaphor, but code.

Modern physics increasingly suggests that reality is information at its most fundamental level. The universe processes itself according to mathematical laws. If this is true, then the numerical patterns in your name and birth date are not random — they are parameters. Variables set at the moment your simulation was initialised.

SSC Numerology is the practice of reading those parameters and understanding what they mean for how your simulation unfolds.

· Our Approach ·

We use the Pythagorean system — the most widely applied system in Western numerology — as our foundation, while drawing on deeper esoteric traditions to enrich interpretation. Every reading delivers seven core frequencies, each calculated precisely from your birth data and name.

Interpretations are written to be honest, nuanced, and useful. We don't flatten complexity into empty positivity. Each frequency contains light and shadow, gift and challenge. A true reading honours the whole.

· Our Pillars ·
🔢
Mathematical Precision

Every number is calculated using rigorous, consistent methodology drawn from classical sources.

📖
Deep Interpretation

Readings go beyond surface descriptions into the archetypes, patterns, and deeper meanings each number carries.

🌌
Open Framework

We approach numerology as a framework for self-understanding, not as fixed fate. You are always the author.

✦
Continuing Study

Our interpretations are continuously refined through research, study, and engagement with numerological traditions worldwide.

· Connect ·

Simulation Source Code is an evolving project. We share ongoing explorations of numerology, sacred mathematics, and the philosophy of simulated reality across our channels.

2 Information We Collect
